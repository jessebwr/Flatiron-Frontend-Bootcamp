Creating our own Contributor Components

  • Start by getting Atrium running locally by following the instructions at https://design.flatiron.io/#/contributing
  • Once it's running, move on to the next slide
  1. Copy one of the existing contributor components in docs/Components/Contributors/People/ to create YourName.js in the same directory
  2. Add your image to the docs/imgs/contributorAvatars/ and update your component to reference it
  3. Reference your component in docs/contributors.js and confirm your component shows up in the Contributor section of Atrium
  4. Update your name, slack id, and add any other changes you want to make to your component!
  5. Once you're done, go on to the next slide to create a pull request
